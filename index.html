About GreenFork

GreenFork is dedicated to reducing food waste by connecting surplus food from merchants to seekers in need. Whether you're a business with excess inventory or an individual looking for affordable meals, we've got you covered. Our smart composting system ensures that unsalvageable food is turned into valuable resources, promoting sustainability.

Food waste in India isn't just an inconvenience—it's a crisis. Edible food is discarded in massive quantities while millions sleep on an empty stomach. GreenFork is a food waste management initiative dedicated to reducing this waste by connecting surplus food from merchants to seekers in need, while composting the rest for a sustainable future.

Our platform minimizes food waste by efficiently redistributing surplus food and repurposing what cannot be consumed. By integrating smart composting and real-time donor-seeker connections, we reduce landfill waste, lower carbon emissions, and support a circular economy—redefining food management for a greener planet.

Developed by students from VESIT, GreenFork combines web development expertise with a passion for sustainability.
