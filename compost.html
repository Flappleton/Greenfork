Smart Composting

When food can't be donated or sold, GreenFork ensures it doesn't go to waste. Our smart composting system turns unsalvageable food into organic fertilizer, reducing landfill waste and methane emissions.

  • IoT-enabled monitoring of temperature, humidity, and oxygen levels.
  • Efficient processing with data-driven insights.
  • Supports a circular economy by repurposing waste for agriculture.
  • Contributes to a sustainable future with every compost batch.
